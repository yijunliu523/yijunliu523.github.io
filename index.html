I'm a first-year Ph.D. student at the Siebel School of Computing and Data Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. I am broadly intrigued by human-AI collaborative technologies that draw inspiration from human-human interaction strategies and emphasize human-in-the-loop collaboration.

I hold a B.S. in Computer Science and a B.S. in Quantitative Sciences with a focus on Sociology from Emory University. During my undergraduate studies, I had the privilege of working with exceptional professors on projects spanning social computing, visualization, and machine learning.
